Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your admission requirements?

We accept children ages 2-6 years. Basic requirements include birth certificate, immunization records, and a brief interaction session. No prior school experience is necessary.

What are your school hours?

We offer flexible schedules: Morning session (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM). Extended care is available for working parents.

Do you provide meals and snacks?

Yes! We provide healthy, nutritious snacks daily. Our certified nutritionist ensures balanced meals that children love. Special dietary requirements are accommodated.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain a maximum ratio of 1:8 for all age groups, ensuring personalized attention and quality care for every child.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we provide safe, reliable transportation services within a 10-mile radius. All vehicles are equipped with safety seats and GPS tracking.

How do you handle discipline?

We use positive discipline techniques focused on teaching and guidance rather than punishment. Our approach helps children develop self-control and responsibility.
